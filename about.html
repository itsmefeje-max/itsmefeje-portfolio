The Architect.

I am a Roblox developer currently focused on the intersection of LuaU architecture and game design. My philosophy is simple: build small, prove the system, then scale.

While many jump straight into building massive worlds, I prefer to start with the mechanics. I build internal prototypes to stress-test gameplay loops, ensuring they are fun before a single asset is finalized.

My goal is to write code that is clean, maintainable, and modular—systems that don't just work today, but scale for the thousands of players of tomorrow.
